The S.P.A.C.E.

The Severity Prevention and Correction Elite (S.P.A.C.E.) are Sci's own spec ops team. They are responsible for correcting any severe errors caused by interactions of multiple realites. Their methods, however, are not perfect, and may occasionally be seen as "plot holes" in certain media.

Archaic

Any attempts to view the future of this individual, whether by subdimensional or superdimensional means, have proven to be ineffective. Additionally, the higher the dimensionality of the attempt, the more force is produced in direct opposion to the attempt.

The Boreal

We have reason to believe that light is not a substance on it's own, but rather, a state of matter acheivable by anything. The closed eye for the boreal has been aquired in order to gather information regarding this. To avoid confusion, the substance regarded as normal light should instead be called photon.

The GLASS Project

The leading members of The GLASS Project are responsible for the development of interspacial travel. Their last status was regarding issues in the stabalization of wormholes, or "flows" in their terminology. Due to such severe deviation from the original intentions of the project, termination of the project is very likely imminent.
